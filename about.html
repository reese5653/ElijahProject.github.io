About Elijah Project School of Ministry

Welcome to Elijah Project School of Ministry - a place dedicated to training and equipping laborers for God's harvest field.

Ronald and Marilyn Green serve as the Pastors of Short Creek Church in Mulga, AL, a little town right outside of Birmingham, Alabama. From a troubled past and many mistakes which eventually led to a 2 and a half year prison sentence, God's love and grace has turned Pastor Ronald Green's life around to inspire others to live passionate lives for Jesus Christ.

After a visitation from the Lord in a county jail cell, God commissioned Ron and Marilyn to raise up Gen Z and millennials to prepare the way for the return of the Lord. Ron and Marilyn are blessed to have many spiritual children while having 7 of their own. Their passion and desire is to see a generation passionately pursue their purpose in God.

Through our 16 comprehensive modules, students will journey from foundational biblical studies to advanced theological concepts, gaining the knowledge and skills needed for effective ministry.
